Bulk sell emits one message per sale, the total as its own message, and the zero-value skip as its own message.

2b emitter audit: buy N — left as one message: the atomic all-or-nothing purchase is a single event, not joined event sentences. The new sweep is per-message by construction. Finding outside scope: repair all also joins its lines into one message, but it predates Brief 3, so per the audit's stated scope I left it alone — it needs its own design-chat ruling (noted in #63's closing comment and the arch doc).
